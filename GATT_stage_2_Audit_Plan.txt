AUDIT OBJECTIVES
The objectives of this audit are: 
Determine conformance of the Management System with the Audit Criteria (see above) 
Evaluate the effectiveness of the Management System in meeting its specified quality objectives 
Evaluate the capability of the Management System to ensure compliance with relevant statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements  
Evaluate the effectiveness of the Management System to ensure that agreed requirements for products and/or services are met 
Identify areas for potential improvement of the Management System
